What is 4ever Songs?

4ever Songs turns stories into beautifully written, high quality recorded songs that are perfect for commemorating milestones. Your first dance, anniversary, a special wedding present; no matter what you want to say (or how you want to say it), we can turn it into a custom song written just for you.


How It Works

Start by telling us your story via the Create Your Song tab. Once you've filled out this questionnaire, that’s all it takes. We'll send you a confirmation email and get to work. We'll produce the music and one of our songwriters will write the lyrics and sing your song. We'll also make an album cover out of photos you send us. You'll receive a link to download your song once it's finished. You'll probably do a little dance when you hear it. This could be the soundtrack to your wedding. Hey, it could even be the soundtrack to your life. It's your story. It's your song. 4ever.
